- **Call booking rate: 20-40% of live attendees** — for high-ticket with call CTA.
- **Closer conversion: ~25% of calls** — stated once. Below 15% = problem.
- **CPL ~4-5€** — depends heavily on niche and market. 8€+ = refresh creatives.
- **ROAS 2.5x minimum to accelerate** — stated clearly. But also mentions 10x, x30 in other contexts.

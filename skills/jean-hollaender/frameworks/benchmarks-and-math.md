## REVERSE ENGINEERING FORMULA: €1M LAUNCH

```
TARGET: 1,000,000€
÷ offer price (e.g., 5,000€)
= 200 sales needed

200 sales ÷ 25% closer conversion
= 800 qualified calls needed

With follow-up (+50%): ~1,000 total calls booked

1,000 calls ÷ 50% call-to-live ratio
= need 1,500-2,000 people LIVE

2,000 live ÷ 25-33% show-up rate
= need ~6,000 REGISTRATIONS

Split: 1,000 organic + 5,000 paid
At ~4€ CPL = ~20,000€ ad budget

EXPECTED ROI: ~50x on ad spend
```

**How Jean uses this:**
Work BACKWARDS from your revenue target. Each number becomes a strategic constraint to optimize.

## SCALING RULES

| Budget Level | Creatives Needed | Team |
|-------------|-----------------|------|
| 50€/jour (démarrage) | 3-5 créatives | Solo |
| 500€/jour | 10-15 créatives | 1-2 copywriters |
| 200K€ total launch | **50 créatives + variants de hooks** | 4-5 copywriters en compétition |

**Copywriter economics:**
- Cost: 100€ per script (30-60 sec ad)
- Performance bonuses on results
- 4-5 copywriters put IN COMPETITION with each other

**Scaling sweet spot:**
Find the maximum lead volume + sales volume for the minimum ad spend. There's a natural ceiling where lead quality degrades.

## SALES TEAM STRUCTURE (High-Ticket Launches)

```
3 Head of Sales (HoS)
├── Team A: ~10 closers (8-10% commission/sale)
├── Team B: ~10 closers (8-10% commission/sale)
└── Team C: ~10 closers (8-10% commission/sale)

HoS commission: 3-5% on total team sales
Competition: teams compete against each other
```

**For weekly models:**
Permanent sales team (not freelance). Example: 100K€/week, 400K€/month.

**Calendars:** Open for 5-6 days after event for call booking.

## HIERARCHY: MARKET > OFFER > MARKETING

Best marketing cannot save a bad offer or bad market.

1. **Market** — Is there demand? Are people paying for this?
2. **Offer** — Is it irresistible? (Error #1 = offer not strong enough)
3. **Marketing** — Only then optimize acquisition and conversion

# Campaign Templates

## How to Use These Templates

These are starting frameworks, NOT copy-paste solutions. Every template must be:
1. Adapted to your specific audience, offer, and voice
2. Personalized with real research (Tiramisu technique)
3. Reviewed before sending (mandatory quality check) [Moubeche principle]

---

## Template 1: Cold Outreach — PAS Framework [Moubeche principle]

### Email 1: The Opener
```
Subject: {{painPoint}} at {{company}}?

Hey {{firstName}},

[TIRAMISU 1: Personalized observation about their business — 1-2 sentences]

[PROBLEM: Name the specific challenge they likely face — 1 sentence]
[AGITATE: Show the cost/impact of this problem — 1 sentence]

[SOLUTION: How you solve this — 1 sentence]
[PROOF: One specific result — 1 sentence]

[CTA: Low-friction, single ask]

[Sign-off]
```

### Email 2: The Value Add (Day +2)
```
[No subject line — same thread]

Hey {{firstName}},

Sharing this because it's directly relevant to {{company}}:

[New angle, resource, or insight — 2-3 sentences]

[Slightly more direct CTA]

[Sign-off]
```

### Email 3: The Proof (Day +6)
```
Hey {{firstName}},

[Similar company] was dealing with [same problem] last quarter.

They [specific action] and [specific quantified result].

If that's on your radar, I can share how in 10 minutes.

[Binary CTA: "This week or next?"]

[Sign-off]
```

### Email 4: The Breakup (Day +15-20)
```
Hey {{firstName}},

I've reached out a few times about [topic] — totally understand if timing is off.

If [problem] is still something {{company}} is working on, happy to help.
If not, no worries at all. Should I reach out to someone else on the team?

[Sign-off]

P.S. [Something light — humor, relevant fact, or personality]
```

## Template 8: Sales — Objection-Handling Email [Inferred extension]

### "We already have a solution for that"
```
Subject: quick thought on {{currentSolution}}

Hey {{firstName}},

Totally get it — {{currentSolution}} is solid for [what it does well].

Quick question: are you happy with {{specific metric}} today,
or is improving that still on the roadmap?

We've seen teams using {{currentSolution}} get [X% better result]
by [specific complementary approach].

Happy to share how — no pitch, just the framework.

[Sign-off]
```

### "Send me more info"
```
What would be most useful:

A) One-page overview of how it works
B) 2 examples for {{use case A}} vs {{use case B}}

I'll send whichever is more relevant to {{company}}.
```

### "Not the right time"
```
Totally understand. When would be better to revisit this?

In the meantime, here's a [resource] on {{topic}} that might be useful
regardless of timing.

[Sign-off]
```


## Template Customization Rules

When adapting any template:
1. Replace ALL placeholder text with real, specific content
2. Add Tiramisu personalization (real research, not generic)
3. Adjust word count to target range (50-125 for cold, etc.)
4. Verify single CTA
5. Check "you/your" ratio (must exceed "I/we/our")
6. Read aloud — if it sounds like a template, rewrite
7. Run through the copy principles checklist
